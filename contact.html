Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring on my adventure?

We provide a detailed packing list for each activity. Generally, you'll need comfortable hiking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, water bottles, and a camera to capture memories.

Are your activities suitable for beginners?

Yes! We offer activities for all skill levels, from beginner-friendly nature walks to advanced hiking trails. Our guides will ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience.

Do you provide equipment?

We provide all necessary safety equipment and some specialized gear. Specific items needed vary by activity, and we'll inform you of what to bring when you book.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation policies. Full refunds are available up to 48 hours before your scheduled activity. Weather-related cancellations are fully refundable.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely! We work with local organic farms and can accommodate various dietary needs. Please inform us of any restrictions when booking.

How do you ensure environmental sustainability?

All our activities follow strict environmental guidelines. We use eco-friendly practices, support local conservation efforts, and educate participants about sustainable tourism.
